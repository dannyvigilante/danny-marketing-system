# Channel Strategy — The Ritual Wellness Club

## Primary Channel: Instagram (@TheRitualPDX)

**Role:** Main brand presence, community hub, event pipeline. Where The Ritual lives between events.

**Content Mix:**
- Stories (daily during event weeks): Behind-the-scenes, route previews, member shoutouts, polls, countdowns
- Feed posts (3-4/week): Event recaps, community photos, philosophy quotes, member spotlights
- Reels (1-2/week): Movement moments, run highlights, Portland scenery, wellness tips
- Carousels: Education content, Art of Wellness deep dives, event photo series

**Posting Cadence:**
- Event week (last week of month): Daily Stories, 4-5 feed posts, 2 Reels
- Off weeks: 2-3 feed posts, 1 Reel, organic Stories
- Brittany manages daily execution; Danny provides strategic direction

**Engagement Rules:**
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- DM responses within 48 hours
- Use member-created content (with permission)
- Never use automated engagement tools
- Follow back real community members, not brands

---

## Secondary Channel: Email Newsletter

**Role:** Deeper connection with committed members. Where philosophy lives.

**Content Mix:**
- Monthly newsletter (mid-month): Upcoming event, Art of Wellness essay, member spotlight, recovery tip
- Event reminders (week of): Logistics, route map, what to bring, weather plan
- Special announcements: Partnerships, gala invitations, merch drops

**Tone:** Like a letter from a friend who runs a really good wellness club.

**Metrics:** Open rate target 40%+, click-through to RSVP, reply rate

---

## Tertiary Channel: Website

**Role:** Digital home base for newcomers.

**Priorities:** Homepage (mission + next event), About (story + team), Events (calendar + RSVP), Community (stories + testimonials)

**Tone:** Most elevated channel. Clean, minimal, confident.

---

## Future Channels

- **TikTok:** Activate when consistent video content exists. Raw, energetic clips.
- **LinkedIn:** Activate for corporate wellness partnerships. Professional but distinctive.
- **Podcast:** Post-gala. Art of Wellness conversations, community stories.

---

## Cross-Channel Rules

1. Visual consistency across all channels (colors, typography, photography style)
2. Voice consistency — tone modulates by channel but voice stays the same
3. No channel-specific messaging conflicts
4. Event is the anchor — every channel drives toward or reflects on the monthly event
5. Community first, content second — never sacrifice a real moment for content
