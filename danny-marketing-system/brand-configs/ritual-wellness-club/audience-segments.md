# Audience Segments — The Ritual Wellness Club

## Primary Audience: The Wellness-Curious Community Seeker

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-45
- Location: Portland metro area
- Income: Mixed — service workers to professionals (accessibility is core)
- Fitness level: Beginner to intermediate (not elite athletes)

**Psychographics:**
- Values community and belonging over competition
- Interested in wellness but turned off by hustle-culture fitness
- Appreciates quality experiences without pretension
- Seeks consistency and ritual, not one-off events
- May have felt excluded by traditional fitness spaces

**What draws them in:**
- "You don't have to be fast to belong" — permission to participate
- The Ritz-Carlton setting — signals quality without requiring qualification
- Monthly cadence — sustainable, not overwhelming
- Real community — not a brand using "community" as marketing language

**What keeps them:**
- Genuine relationships formed through repeated attendance
- The feeling of being part of something intentional
- Visible diversity in the group — not performative, actual
- Quality of the experience (route selection, post-run gathering, coaching)

---

## Secondary Audience: The Wellness-Adjacent Professional

**Demographics:**
- Age: 30-50
- Location: Portland metro, potentially remote for special events
- Income: Mid to high
- Fitness level: Moderate (already has some practice)

**Psychographics:**
- Already invests in wellness (yoga, meditation apps, gym membership)
- Looking for the community component missing from solo practice
- Drawn to elevated, curated experiences
- Values authenticity — can detect performative wellness instantly

**What draws them in:**
- The "silent luxury" positioning aligns with their taste
- Events feel curated, not mass-produced
- Fills a gap their gym or studio doesn't — real human connection
- The Ritz-Carlton partnership signals a standard they trust

**What keeps them:**
- The philosophy resonates (practice, not performance)
- Events consistently deliver
- They bring friends, deepening their own investment

---

## Tertiary Audience: The Portland Local Explorer

**Demographics:**
- Age: 21-55, Location: Portland, Income: Variable, Fitness level: Any

**Psychographics:**
- Loves discovering Portland's community scene
- Interested in unique local experiences
- May not identify as a "wellness" person but is open to it

**What draws them in:** Word of mouth, Instagram content showing real Portland moments, novelty of wellness at The Ritz-Carlton, low barrier to entry

**What converts them:** The experience exceeds expectations, they feel welcomed, the format is repeatable

---

## Anti-Audience (Who We're NOT For)

- **Competitive athletes** looking for performance optimization
- **Wellness influencers** looking for content backdrop
- **Luxury consumers** who want exclusivity as a feature
- **Quick-fix seekers** looking for transformation promises

We don't push these people away, but our messaging won't resonate — and that's by design.
